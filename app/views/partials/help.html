Welcome to RektGG

About us

We are two league of legends fanatics who love everything about the game and as fellow players we are aware of some major issues that players face. To address one of the concerns we decided to build RektGG which gives players the ability to share their experiences with other players. Our end goal is to encourage a more positive game environment.

Contact us: RektGGtemp@Gmail.com

General Questions

What is the purpose of this site?

Ever felt that reporting or honoring was never enough? RektGG allows users to go into detail and share, not just with riot but with other players, their experiences they had with certain summoners.

Registerd users will also have their own profiles where they may post anything that comes to their minds, this will be expanded (see "What the future holds")

How do I review players?

You must register with our site to review other players, once registered you will be able to rate and review a summoner after searching their summoner name.

Will the ratings have any effect on my league of legends account?

No, the ratings and reviews are strictly informational. Players will have hold various opinions of a summoner but patterns may be identified to help direct the RektGG user on how to approach and respond to certain summoners.

What the future holds

Based on feedback we will implement more features but these are the ideas we have planned.

Friend list

We plan to allow registerd users to add other registered users or verified summoners (see "Account verification") to their personal friendlist to enable quick and easy access to their profiles. Our goal for this feature is to make RektGG a social platform where users can share their amazing plays, funny moments, creative builds, and everything else they enjoy about league

Account verification

We will allow summoners to link their accounts to their profiles so that they will have the options of both user and summoner. These options include Matches, Reviews, Activity, and Friends. Our goal for this feature is for simplicity, users will be able to see their summoner profile page while still having the benefits of a user profile page.

Profile personalization

We also want to enhance the user experience and plan to allow users to modify their profile images, background banner, usernames, avatar, and more. Our goal for this feature is to make users feel that they have their own personal space where they can share anything they wish.
